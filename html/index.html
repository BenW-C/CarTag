Cartag, the concept.

drivers from all over Belgium make the rear window of their car available for stickers. This enables companies to advertise geographically in a very targeted manner. Cartag matches the correct drivers to the brand. The Drivers receive a monthly fee for proven journeys. the companies gain name recognition in their chosen region.
